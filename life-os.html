Anxiety Relief Exercises
5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Name 5 things you see → 4 you can touch → 3 you hear → 2 you smell → 1 you taste. Anchors you to the present moment.

Physiological Sigh

Double-inhale through nose (two quick sniffs), then long slow exhale through mouth. Repeat 2-3 times. Fastest way to calm down.

Cognitive Defusion

Say "I notice I'm having the thought that..." before any anxious thought. Creates distance between you and the thought.

Cold Water Reset

Splash cold water on your face or hold cold water. Triggers the dive reflex, slowing heart rate within 30 seconds.

Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Tense each muscle group for 5s, then release for 30s. Start at feet, work up to face. Releases physical tension from anxiety.

Worry Journaling

Write your worry, then ask: Is this in my control? What's the realistic outcome? What would I tell a friend? Externalizes the spiral.
